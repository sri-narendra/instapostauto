# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creators, social media operators, and small teams who need a high-impact carousel post from a short prompt without opening a design tool.

## Product Purpose

TextToPost turns a prompt into a polished square-social carousel. Success means the output feels deliberately sequenced and art-directed, not like a random pile of effects.

## Brand Personality

Bold, crafted, cinematic.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI poster sludge, random cyber HUD clutter, pure black and pure white defaults, gradient text, and decorative glassmorphism.

## Design Principles

- Make one strong typographic idea carry each slide.
- Use procedural detail as atmosphere, not noise.
- Let cultural or topical prompts influence tone without falling into the first obvious palette.
- Keep the generator easy to split, test, and reshape.
- Favor designed restraint over maximum decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Generated carousel slides should preserve readable contrast, avoid tiny body copy, and respect reduced visual complexity where possible.
